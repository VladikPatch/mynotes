Instructions to Delete Your User Account

To request the deletion of your user account, please follow these steps:

1. Send an email to patchstudiocompanyv@gmail.com.

2. Use the subject line: "Account Deletion Request".

3. Include the following details in your email:

  • Your registered username or email address
  • A brief statement confirming you want your account deleted

Data Deletion Notice:

By requesting to delete your account, all your personal information, including your email, password, and stored notes, will be permanently removed from our system.

Your data will remain stored until you decide to delete it or after an extended period of inactivity.

If you wish to proceed with deletion, please ensure this is your final decision.
